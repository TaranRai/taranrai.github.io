  • NLP internship ü§ñ @ Research Centre for Machine Learning, City, University of London

    Jul 2017

    In my first venture into NLP, I augmented Neural Language Models by integrating Parts-Of-Speech (POS) tags into LSTM and GRU recurrent neural network architectures, leveraging valuable structural information.   • PhD program @ CVSSP, University of Surrey üéì

    Feb 2018

    Undoubtedly the most significant and challenging phase of my career, I received a fully funded studentship from Zoetis and NPL, leading me to embark on a PhD journey at CVSSP. Under the guidance of Dr. Kevin Wells, Prof. Miroslaw Bober, Dr. Spencer Angus Thomas, and Prof. Roberto La Ragione, I delved into multiple projects centered around AI/ML, computer vision, and diagnostic analysis of biopsy images. Over the course of nearly five years, I acquired invaluable scientific and technical knowledge, presenting and published extensively at renowned medical imaging conferences worldwide as well as journals. My works can be found on Google Scholar and ResearchGate. Personally and professionally, this period was an absolute rollercoaster.
